# P7 Science deepening pass — 2026-07-01

## Verdict

P7 Integrated Science has been deepened beyond the earlier complete-enough state. It now has stronger classroom/application depth across all 8 official NCDC topics.

As with English, Science should not be judged only by top-level topic count. The official NCDC Science outline has 8 broad topics; the product-depth standard is whether each topic supports explanation, observation, application, diagrams/experiments, safety and practice.

### 2. Guided Science tasks added for all 8 topics
Science now has guided application tasks for:

1. Muscular-skeletal system
   - observation task: good posture and movement
2. Electricity and magnetism
   - experiment plan: making an electromagnet
3. Energy resources in the environment
   - community application: choosing an energy resource
4. Simple machines and friction
   - application task: choosing a simple machine
5. Excretory system
   - explanation task: keeping excretory organs healthy
6. Light energy
   - ray diagram explanation: reflection and shadows
7. Interdependence of things in the environment
   - food chain task: interdependence in a garden
8. Population and health
   - health advice task: preventing disease in a community

Each guided task includes:
- prompt
- planning steps
- checklist
- model opening
