## A structured study programme for every upper-primary student

Tendo is a school-branded web platform that helps upper-primary students study for the PLE across Mathematics, English, Integrated Science, and Social Studies. It gives students structured notes, worked examples, auto-marked quizzes, and real past papers on any phone.

## Why this matters

The PLE syllabus is knowable and finite. Students who study every topic with the right questions and feedback are better prepared. We are building Tendo to make that study path calm, structured, and easy for every school to offer its parents.

## What students get

- **Watch and Read** on every topic. Video explainers for high-stakes topics, text notes for all others.
- **Worked examples** written in plain language with Ugandan contexts.
- **Auto-marked quizzes** with instant feedback after every topic.
- **Endless practice** with a different question every time.
- **Past PLE papers** to attempt as a student, with a score and review.

## What teachers get

- **Worksheet generator** to mix topics, set difficulty, and print in two clicks.
- **Browse-by-topic past papers** to find relevant questions fast.
- **Class dashboard** to see which topics need re-teaching and drill down to any student.
- **Teacher Fellowship** so senior teachers earn recognition for improving content.

## What the school gets

- A school-branded page, ready for your parents to see.
- Zero IT burden: no installation, no server, no support tickets.
- A termly usage report showing what students have studied.
- A clear, credible answer to parents asking, "What extra are you doing for PLE?"

## What we are looking for

We are looking for partner schools to pilot Tendo with a single P7 class this term. We want to work closely with your teachers and students to make sure the platform is genuinely useful before we expand the rollout.

## The pilot

- One P7 class of your choice. We set up a branded page for your school in five working days.
- We provide a short message you can share with parents. At the end of the term we review what worked and what to improve.
- No cost for the pilot term.
