## A structured revision programme for every P7 student

Tendo is a school-branded web platform that helps P7 students master the entire UNEB Mathematics syllabus through structured notes, worked examples, auto-marked quizzes, and real past PLE papers. Your school offers it to parents as part of your revision programme. We handle the technology; you keep the relationship and the margin.

> **Why this matters:** The PLE Mathematics syllabus is finite — 6 themes, 11 topics, 40 sub-topics. Students who practice every sub-topic with real questions are prepared. Tendo turns that syllabus into a calm, phone-friendly revision path.

---

## What students get

- **Watch or Read** every topic — video explainers for high-stakes topics, text notes for all others.
- **Worked examples** written in plain language with Ugandan contexts.
- **Auto-marked quizzes** with instant feedback after every topic.
- **Endless practice** — a different question every time from a 200+ question bank.
- **Past PLE papers** — attempt as a student, get an exam-style score and review.

## What teachers get

- **Worksheet generator** — mix topics, set length and difficulty, print in two clicks.
- **Browse-by-topic past papers** — find relevant questions without flipping through booklets.
- **Class dashboard** — see which topics need re-teaching, drill down to any student, export reports to parents.
- **Teacher Fellowship** — senior teachers earn public recognition for reviewing and improving content.

## What the school gets

- A school-branded subdomain, e.g. **tendo-your-school.ug**.
- Zero IT burden: no installation, no server, no support tickets.
- A usage report at the end of the term.
- A credible answer to parents asking, "What extra are you doing for PLE?"
